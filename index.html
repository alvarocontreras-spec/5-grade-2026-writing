My Daily Routine

A2 English Writing Task - 5th Year

🎯 Goal: Talk about your daily routine. Use Present Simple to describe 5-6 actions.
📝 Instructions:
  • Write 5–6 sentences.
  • Range: 50 - 60 words.
  • Use: and, but, because.
🧱 Vocabulary Box:
Morning, afternoon, night, lunch, school, home, study, watch TV, happy, fun.
📌 Model (Do not copy!):
I wake up at 7:00. I go to school in the morning. I eat lunch at school. In the afternoon, I play football. I like football because it is fun. I go to bed at 10:00.
